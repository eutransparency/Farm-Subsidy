What are lists?

Lists allow you to put together groups of recipients. Lists work just like a shopping cart or an Amazon.com wish list. Begin making a list and keep adding recipients. Saving a list means you can come back to later and the list can be viewed by other users.

You can make any kind of list you like. You could make a list of large multinational companies, politicians getting farm subsidies, or farms in your local area. Here are some examples of lists.
