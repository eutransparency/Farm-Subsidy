What are lists?

Lists allow you to put together groups of recipients. Lists work just like an online shopping cart or an Amazon.com wish list. Once you begin making a list you can keep on adding as many recipients as you like to that list. You can use a list as a simple way of adding payments made to multiple recipients or you can save and publish your list for other people to see. Saving a list also means you can come back to it later.

You can make any kind of list you like. You could make a list of large multinational companies, politicians getting farm subsidies, or farms in your local area.
