What are lists?

You can think of custom lists as a little like a shopping cart or an Amazon.com wish list: while browsing the website you can add ‘items’ (subsidy recipients) to your list like you would on any shopping website. You can then save the list so you can access it at a later date, or publish it for others to see.

You can give your list a title and a description. We hope that lists will be a new way for people to share their own expertise and analysis.

For instance, if you have special knowledge about certain vessels that are fishing for an especially threatened fish stock, you might like to make a custom list of those vessels. Or if you know of a number of vessels that are all owned by the same company, you might make a list of them.
